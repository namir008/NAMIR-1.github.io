ExamTrainer

During the covid 19 lockdown, I used a databank of past examination questions and answers used for one of my company's products to develop a classifier that uses TF-IDF to identify patterns and relevant keywords/similarities in questions then classify those questions using SVM. The goal was to identify the topics(distinct classifications) that the student was weak in and get related questions from the databank then push those to the student as a sort of training guide.
